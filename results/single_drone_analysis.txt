All test scenarios completed successfully.
Results demonstrate vortex cannon engagement capabilities across
a wide range of target types, positions, and movement patterns.

Key findings:
- Optimal engagement range: 15-35 meters
- Effective against small-medium drones: P_kill > 0.6
- Moving target interception: Successful with lead angle calculation
- Performance degrades with range and target size
- System shows clear operational envelope boundaries
